Project Details

Oink is a hyper casual game that sheds light on the effects of the meat industry on the environment. You need to save livestock from being eaten by people by breaking into slaughterhouses and leading them to safety.

I was lead designer and created the bigger part of our models and elements in MagicaVoxel and Photoshop. I also designed our logo and graphical profile for branding. Originally, the game was in 8-bit design but we slowly moved away to a mixed look.

As a developer, I implemented our onboarding menu in Unity.

  • Role: Lead Designer, developer
  • Using: Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, MagicaVoxel, Unity 3D Engine
  • Team size: 2
  • Course: Computer Game Design
