Personal History

As far as I can remember, I started playing video games. This was a gateway for interacting with technology and has set me upon the path I am today.

Developing as a creative, I developed interests in dancing, art and photography. In time I discovered design and front end development during my studies. In my master, I came into contact with making virtual reality games.

Education

Originally pursuing a career in dancing, I started studying at The Royal Institute of Technology (KTH), to pursue my interest in technology. Starting out in Computer Science for two years, I moved on for a full M.Sc.Eng, Degree Program in Media Technology. My masters specialised in interactive media technology with a focus on interaction and graphics in games.

Experience

I started my first internship when I was studying Computer Science, at one of Sweden's newspaper Dagens Nyheter, Expressen and Dagens Industri on Bonnier News.

At university, I worked as a teaching assistant in Applied Computer Science. Meanwhile, I worked part time at Digpro Technologies as a front end developer and UI designer. Here, I mainly used Adobe XD and HTML/CSS/Javascript to stetch interfaces, wireframes and to develop web pages.

Today, I am a Master Thesis Intern at Resolution Games and my thesis is focusing on VR development.
