Project Details

We wanted to create a VR fishing simulator using HTC Vive and Unity. Initially, I was responsible for the technical side of interactions and he fishing rod gameplay. I implemented interactions with objects such as fishing rod functionality, natural fishing line physics and spawning fishes for the gameplay.

I also took on rigging and animating fishes in Blender, which I used for the first time here, and our game poster. We exhibited the game in its early stage at the event Friday for Scientists (KTH:s Forskarfredag) and the final version at the Technical Museum of Stockholm (Tekniska Museet). We are satisfied with the results as we had barely 1.5 months to put it together on top of other courses and managing the branding of the game.

  • Role: Developer, Animator, Designer
  • Using: Unity 3D Engine, Blender 2.7b, Photoshop
  • Team size: 6
  • Course: Advanced Interaction and Graphics
