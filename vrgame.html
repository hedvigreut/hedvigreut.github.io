MULTIMODAL INTERACTION

THE ULTIMATE MAGICAL VR GAME

ROLE: DEVELOPER

ABOUT THE PROJECT

In DT2120 Multimodal Interaction and interfaces Interactive technology, me and my group chose to create a VR game with speech recognition. Being a fan of magical worlds such as Harry Potter, I came up with an idea of eliminating obstacles and enemies by using the voice activation.

The player is inside a big library where enemies are being spawned faster and faster. The player must eliminate these by a set of voice commands. For example, when a door is being spawned the player must say "Open!", if a dementor appears and hovers towards the player the player must say "Go away!" and lastly, if a dark lord comes at you the player must say "Destroy!".

The enemies with eventually block the players view, preventing the player from seeing which objects that are spawned (though the player can hear them) and the library will be overrun by enemies.

I was a developer and designer. This game was my baby.

We used Unity, SteamVR and Windows speech.
